=== (S.5) Prioritization

ifdef::env-draft[]
TIP: _Classification of the behaviors, interfaces and scenarios (<<s2>>, <<s3>> and <<s4>>) by their degree of criticality. It is useful in particular if during the course of the project various pressures force the team to drop certain functions._  <<BM22>>
endif::[]

==== Critical (Must-Have)
* Fragment search, filtering, and placement on the canvas (S.4.1, S.4.2) 
** enables the core reconstruction workflow.
* Canvas manipulation (move, resize, rotate, lock) (S.4.2) 
** required for arranging fragments accurately.
* Project save and restore (S.4.3) 
** preserves reconstruction state across sessions.

==== Important (Should-Have)
* Metadata viewing and editing (S.4.4) 
** supports curation and correction of fragment metadata.
* Uploading new fragments (S.4.5) 
** allows expanding the dataset.
** populates segmentation and metadata fields used by filters and display.
* Custom filters (S.4.2, S.4.4) 
** supports project-specific metadata needs.

==== Optional (Nice-to-Have)
* Edge matching prototype outputs (S.4.6) 
** useful for research exploration but not integrated into the UI.
* Manual scale setting from canvas resize (S.4.4) 
** helpful for calibration when auto-detection fails.
